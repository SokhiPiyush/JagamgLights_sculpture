WHO ARE WE

Welcome to Sculpture, a revolutionary decorative lighting company based in India! We specialize in manufacturing kinetic and dynamic decorative lights that surpass the traditional boundaries of lighting design. Our aim is to create lights that are not only functional but also a work of art.

At Sculpture, we believe that lighting is not just about illuminating a space, but it's also about creating an ambiance that reflects the individuality and style of the people who inhabit that space. That's why we have dedicated ourselves to creating decorative lights that are more than just a source of light. Our lights are designed to change their color, shape, size, and form to match any design, mood, or occasion.
